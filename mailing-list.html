ESGF Mailing List

Subscribing to the mailing list for users

To join the users mailing list, send an email to majordomo@lists.llnl.gov with the following in the body:

subscribe esgf-user

(For the developers mailing list, see the developer info page.)

Remember: the best place to ask and anwser any questions regarding ESGF (installing, publishing, front-end, etc.) is our question/answer forum!
